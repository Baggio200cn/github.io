ABSTRACT
The emergence of Large Language Models (LLMs) has fundamentally transformed natural language processing, making them indispensable across domains ranging from conversational systems to scientific exploration. However, their pre-trained architectures often reveal limitations in specialized contexts, including restricted reasoning capacities, ethical uncertainties, and suboptimal domain-specific performance. These challenges necessitate advanced post-training language models (PoLMs) to address these shortcomings, such as OpenAI-o1/o3 and DeepSeek-R1 (collectively known as Large Reasoning Models, or LRMs).
摘要
大型语言模型（LLMs）的出现彻底改变了自然语言处理领域，使其在对话系统到科学探索等多个场景中不可或缺。然而，其预训练架构在专业场景中常显露出局限性，包括推理能力受限、伦理不确定性及领域性能欠佳等问题【后训练语言模型（PoLMs）：指在预训练后通过微调、对齐等方法优化的模型，如OpenAI-o1/o3和DeepSeek-R1（统称大型推理模型LRMs）[28][41]】。

Language models (LMs) [1, 2] represent sophisticated computational frameworks designed to model and generate human language. These models have revolutionized the field of natural language processing (NLP) [3] by enabling machines to understand, generate, and interact with human language in a manner that closely mimics human cognition.

2. Overview

2.1 History of PoLMs

The advancement of LLMs constitutes a pivotal chapter in natural language processing (NLP), with post-training methods serving as critical catalysts in their evolution from generalized pre-trained architectures to specialized, task-adaptive systems.

2. 概述

2.1 PoLMs发展史

LLMs的演进是自然语言处理（NLP）的关键篇章，后训练方法作为催化剂推动其从通用预训练架构发展为专业化、任务自适应的系统【任务自适应（Task-Adaptive）：模型通过微调等技术适应特定任务需求[16][17]】。

3. PoLMs for Fine-Tuning

3.1 Supervised Fine-Tuning

Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) [45] adapts pre-trained LLMs to specific tasks by leveraging task-specific labeled datasets. Distinct from instruction tuning, which relies on directive prompts, SFT directly adjusts model parameters using annotated data.

3. 微调技术

3.1 监督微调

监督微调（SFT）[45]通过任务特定标注数据集适配预训练LLMs。与依赖指令提示的指令调优不同，SFT直接通过标注数据调整模型参数【监督微调（SFT）：使用标注数据直接优化模型参数的微调方法[45]】。

4. PoLMs for Alignment

4.1 Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback

RLHF first aggregates human feedback in the form of preference labels or reward signals, then uses that information to train a reward model.

4. 对齐技术

4.1 基于人类反馈的强化学习（RLHF）

RLHF首先收集人类偏好标签或奖励信号形式的反馈，随后利用该信息训练奖励模型【强化学习人类反馈（RLHF）：通过人类反馈构建奖励模型以对齐模型行为的框架[45][104]】。

5. PoLMs for Reasoning

5.1 Self-Refine for Reasoning

Self-refine constitutes a powerful mechanism to iteratively pinpoint and correct errors during or after text generation, substantially improving both reasoning depth and overall reliability. As shown in Fig. 12, self-refine methods can be divided into four categories: Intrinsic Self-refine, which relies on the model’s internal reasoning loops; External Self-refine, which incorporates external feedback resources; Fine-tuned Intrinsic Self-refine, which iteratively updates the model’s reasoning processes based on self-generated corrections; and Fine-tuned External Self-refine, which harnesses external signals and fine-tuning to refine reasoning in a more adaptive, long-term manner.

5. 推理增强

5.1 自优化推理

自优化（Self-Refine）是一种通过迭代定位并修正文本生成过程中或生成后的错误来显著提升推理深度与整体可靠性的机制。如图12所示，自优化方法可分为四类：

  1. 1. 内部自优化（Intrinsic Self-refine）：依赖模型内部推理循环；
  2. 2. 外部自优化（External Self-refine）：整合外部反馈资源；
  3. 3. 微调型内部自优化：基于自我生成的修正迭代更新推理过程；
  4. 4. 微调型外部自优化：利用外部信号与微调实现长期自适应优化【自优化（Self-Refine）：通过模型自我修正提升推理能力的方法，分为内部循环与外部反馈两类[164][177]】。

Intrinsic Self-Refine
Intrinsic self-refine methods focus on empowering the model itself to detect and fix errors internally without resorting to outside tools. For instance, RCI Prompting [190] only triggers corrections when a contradiction or error is identified, avoiding overreactions to minor uncertainties.

内部自优化
内部自优化方法使模型能够自主检测并修正错误，无需外部工具。例如，RCI提示法（RCI Prompting）[190]仅在检测到矛盾或错误时触发修正，避免对细微不确定性过度反应【RCI提示法（RCI Prompting）：通过条件触发的自修正机制减少冗余优化[190]】。

External Self-Refine
These methods involve extrinsic feedback sources or computational tools to guide and correct the model’s reasoning. For example, CRITIC [177] systematically checks step-by-step outputs, enhancing the reliability of complex reasoning tasks.

外部自优化
此类方法通过外部反馈源或计算工具引导和修正模型推理。例如，CRITIC框架[177]系统化校验逐步输出，提升复杂推理任务的可靠性【CRITIC：结合外部验证工具（如代码执行器）的推理修正框架[177]】。

5.2 Reinforcement Learning for Reasoning

Reinforcement Learning (RL) provides a framework for training LLMs to generate coherent multi-step reasoning chains by optimizing long-term reward signals. DeepSeek-R1 [28] exemplifies this paradigm, employing Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) to enhance mathematical and logical reasoning capabilities.

5.2 强化学习推理

强化学习（RL）通过优化长期奖励信号，为训练LLMs生成连贯的多步推理链提供框架。DeepSeek-R1[28]采用组相对策略优化（GRPO）增强数学与逻辑推理能力，是该范式的典型代表【组相对策略优化（GRPO）：通过组内奖励对比降低计算开销的RL算法[28][114]】。

Formulating Reasoning as an MDP
Reasoning is modeled as a Markov Decision Process (MDP) ( \mathcal{M} = (\mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P, R, \gamma) ), where:

  • • State Space ( \mathcal{S} ): Represents the current reasoning context, including prior steps and partial conclusions.
  • • Action Space ( \mathcal{A} ): Includes generating a reasoning step or selecting a logical rule.
  • • Reward Function ( R ): Evaluates step-wise correctness and final answer accuracy.

推理的MDP建模
推理被建模为马尔可夫决策过程（MDP）( \mathcal{M} = (\mathcal{S}, \mathcal{A}, P, R, \gamma) )，其中：

  • • 状态空间 ( \mathcal{S} )：包含当前推理上下文（如已生成的步骤与部分结论）；
  • • 动作空间 ( \mathcal{A} )：包括生成推理步骤或选择逻辑规则；
  • • 奖励函数 ( R )：评估步骤正确性与最终答案准确性【马尔可夫决策过程（MDP）：描述序列决策问题的数学框架，包含状态、动作、转移概率与奖励[200]】。

Reward Design for Reasoning
The reward function combines dense signals (e.g., validity of intermediate steps) and sparse signals (e.g., final answer correctness). For DeepSeek-R1-Zero, rewards are derived from rule-based checks and self-consistency across multiple reasoning paths.

推理奖励设计
奖励函数融合密集信号（如中间步骤有效性）与稀疏信号（如最终答案正确性）。DeepSeek-R1-Zero通过基于规则的校验与多推理路径自洽性生成奖励【自洽性奖励（Self-Consistency Reward）：通过多条推理路径的一致性评估可靠性的方法[28]】。

6. PoLMs for Efficiency

6.1 Model Compression

6.1.1 Post-training Quantization

Quantization reduces model precision (e.g., from 32-bit to 8-bit) to decrease memory usage. GPTQ [202] applies layer-wise quantization, while SpQR [201] combines sparse patterns for efficient inference.

6. 效率优化

6.1 模型压缩

6.1.1 后训练量化

量化通过降低数值精度（如32位浮点到8位整型）减少内存占用。GPTQ[202]采用分层量化，而SpQR[201]结合稀疏模式提升推理效率【后训练量化（PTQ）：在模型训练后降低参数精度的压缩技术[201][202]】。

7. PoLMs for Integration and Adaptation

7.1 Multi-Modal Integration

Multi-modal integration equips LLMs to process heterogeneous data types (e.g., text, images, audio) through unified architectures. As depicted in Fig. 18, typical large multi-modal models (LMMs) consist of three core components: a modality encoder, a pre-trained LLM backbone, and a modality connector.

7. 集成与适应

7.1 多模态集成

多模态集成使LLMs能够通过统一架构处理异构数据类型（如文本、图像、音频）。如图18所示，典型的大型多模态模型（LMMs）包含三个核心组件：模态编码器、预训练LLM主干和模态连接器【大型多模态模型（LMMs）：能处理多种输入模态（如图文音）的扩展型LLMs[50][326]】。

7.1.1 Modal Connection

Modal connection methods synthesize multi-modal data into a unified representational space. These are categorized into three strategies: projection-based, query-based, and fusion-based approaches (Fig. 19).

7.1.1 模态连接

模态连接方法将多模态数据融合至统一表征空间，分为三类策略：投影式、查询式和融合式（图19）【模态连接（Modal Connection）：对齐不同模态特征以实现跨模态交互的技术[328]】。

Projection-based Connection
This approach aligns multi-modal features into the LLM's text embedding space. For example, LLaMA-Adapter [329] injects visual tokens into early transformer layers via zero-initialized attention mechanisms, enabling image-conditioned text generation.

投影式连接
该方法将多模态特征对齐至LLM的文本嵌入空间。例如，LLaMA-Adapter[329]通过零初始化注意力机制将视觉标记注入Transformer浅层，实现图像条件化文本生成【零初始化注意力（Zero-Init Attention）：初始权重为零的注意力模块，避免干扰预训练知识[329]】。

Query-based Connection
Methods like Flamingo [51] use cross-attention layers to let the LLM query visual features from a frozen vision encoder (e.g., CLIP [332]), achieving dynamic modality interaction.

查询式连接
Flamingo[51]等模型通过交叉注意力层使LLM从冻结的视觉编码器（如CLIP[332]）查询视觉特征，实现动态模态交互【交叉注意力（Cross-Attention）：在Transformer中实现跨模态特征交互的机制[51]】。

Fusion-based Connection
BLIP-2 [52] combines Q-Former (Querying Transformer) to bridge vision and language encoders, while LLaVA [53] employs linear projection matrices for feature alignment.

融合式连接
BLIP-2[52]通过Q-Former（查询式Transformer）桥接视觉与语言编码器，而LLaVA[53]使用线性投影矩阵实现特征对齐【Q-Former：通过可学习查询向量提取跨模态特征的模块[52]】。

7.1.2 Modal Encoder

Modality-specific encoders (e.g., vision transformers for images) extract features preserved during post-training. For instance, Qwen-VL [333] fine-tunes a ViT-G [334] encoder to maintain visual semantics while adapting to text generation.

7.1.2 模态编码器

模态专用编码器（如视觉Transformer处理图像）在训练后保留特征提取能力。例如，Qwen-VL[333]微调ViT-G[334]编码器以保持视觉语义，同时适配文本生成任务【视觉Transformer（ViT）：将图像分块编码为序列的视觉特征提取器[334]】。

7.2 Domain Adaptation

7.2.1 Knowledge Editing

Knowledge editing updates LLMs to reflect new domain-specific facts without full retraining. ROME [335] modifies specific model parameters via rank-one decomposition, while MEMIT [336] scales this to edit thousands of factual associations.

7.2 领域适应

7.2.1 知识编辑

知识编辑通过局部参数更新使LLMs适应新领域知识，无需完全重训练。ROME[335]通过秩一分解修改特定参数，MEMIT[336]扩展该方法至大规模事实关联编辑【秩一分解（Rank-One Decomposition）：通过低秩矩阵调整模型权重的参数编辑技术[335]】。

7.2.2 Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

RAG enhances domain-specific responses by dynamically retrieving external knowledge. REALM [337] trains a retriever jointly with the LLM, while Atlas [338] uses frozen retrievers for zero-shot adaptation.

7.2.2 检索增强生成（RAG）

RAG通过动态检索外部知识增强领域特定响应。REALM[337]联合训练检索器与LLM，Atlas[338]使用冻结检索器实现零样本适应【检索增强生成（RAG）：结合外部数据库检索的生成框架[48][337]】。

7.3 Model Merging

7.3.1 Model Merging at Hierarchical Levels

Model merging combines parameters from multiple LLMs to enhance capabilities. Layer-wise merging (e.g., model soups [339]) averages weights across layers, while task arithmetic [340] adds task-specific delta weights.

7.3 模型融合

7.3.1 层级融合

模型融合通过整合多个LLMs的参数提升能力。层级融合（如模型缝合[339]）对各层权重取平均，任务算术[340]则添加任务特定增量权重【模型缝合（Model Soups）：集成多个微调模型权重的融合方法[339]】。

Pre-Merging Methods
TIES-Merging [341] resolves parameter conflicts via trimming and rescaling, whereas DARE [342] drops redundant parameters before merging.

预融合方法
TIES-Merging[341]通过修剪与重缩放解决参数冲突，DARE[342]在融合前丢弃冗余参数【参数修剪（Parameter Trimming）：移除模型中冗余或冲突权重的技术[341]】。

During-Merging Methods
Task Vector Arithmetic [343] linearly combines task-specific vectors, while Franckenstein [344] merges attention heads across models.

融合中方法
任务向量算术[343]线性组合任务特定向量，Franckenstein[344]跨模型融合注意力头【任务向量（Task Vector）：表征模型在特定任务上参数变化的向量[343]】。

8. Datasets

8.1 Human-Labeled Datasets

Human-labeled datasets provide high-quality annotations for supervised fine-tuning and evaluation. Examples include SQuAD [345] for question answering and HotpotQA [346] for multi-hop reasoning. Crowdsourcing platforms (e.g., Amazon Mechanical Turk) and expert annotations are primary data sources.

8. 数据集

8.1 人工标注数据集

人工标注数据集为监督微调和评估提供高质量标注。例如，SQuAD[345]用于问答任务，HotpotQA[346]用于多跳推理。众包平台（如Amazon Mechanical Turk）和专家标注是主要数据来源【众包标注（Crowdsourcing Annotation）：通过分布式劳动力完成数据标注的方法[345][346]】。

Challenges in Human Labeling
Labeler bias and inconsistency are key challenges. Techniques like majority voting and adjudication by domain experts mitigate these issues, as seen in the creation of SuperGLUE [347].

人工标注的挑战
标注者偏见与不一致性是核心挑战。多数投票和领域专家仲裁（如SuperGLUE[347]的构建）可缓解此类问题【多数投票（Majority Voting）：通过多标注者投票确定最终标签的质量控制方法[347]】。

8.2 Distilled Dataset

Distilled datasets transfer knowledge from teacher LLMs (e.g., GPT-4) to student models via synthetic examples. The DeepSeek-R1 dataset [28] contains 800K samples, including 600K reasoning paths generated by self-refinement loops.

8.2 蒸馏数据集

蒸馏数据集通过教师LLMs（如GPT-4）生成的合成样本向学生模型迁移知识。DeepSeek-R1数据集[28]包含80万样本，其中60万为自优化循环生成的推理路径【知识蒸馏（Knowledge Distillation）：通过教师模型生成数据训练学生模型的技术[28][295]】。

8.3 Synthetic Datasets

Synthetic datasets are algorithmically generated without human intervention. Self-Instruct [86] uses LLMs to create instruction-output pairs, while Evol-Instruct [348] applies evolutionary algorithms to enhance data diversity.

8.3 合成数据集

合成数据集通过算法自动生成，无需人工参与。Self-Instruct[86]利用LLMs生成指令-输出对，Evol-Instruct[348]通过进化算法提升数据多样性【进化算法（Evolutionary Algorithm）：模拟自然选择优化数据生成的策略[348]】。

9. Applications

9.1 Professional Domains

LLMs excel in legal document analysis, medical diagnosis support, and financial forecasting. For example, Med-PaLM 2 [349] achieves expert-level accuracy on USMLE-style medical exams.

9. 应用场景

9.1 专业领域

LLMs在法律文书分析、医疗诊断支持与金融预测中表现卓越。例如，Med-PaLM 2[349]在美国医师执照考试（USMLE）类试题中达到专家级准确率【USMLE：美国医师执照考试，用于评估医学知识[349]】。

9.2 Technical and Logical Reasoning

Models like DeepSeek-R1 [28] and GPT-4 [9] solve complex mathematical proofs and programming challenges (e.g., Codeforces problems).

9.2 技术与逻辑推理

DeepSeek-R1[28]与GPT-4[9]等模型可解决复杂数学证明与编程挑战（如Codeforces竞赛题）【Codeforces：国际编程竞赛平台，题目以高难度著称[28]】。

9.3 Understanding and Interaction

LLMs power conversational agents (e.g., ChatGPT) and multimodal systems (e.g., GPT-4V [350]) that integrate text, image, and voice interactions.

9.3 理解与交互

LLMs驱动对话代理（如ChatGPT）和多模态系统（如GPT-4V[350]），实现文本、图像与语音的整合交互【多模态系统（Multimodal System）：支持多种输入/输出模态的AI系统[350]】。

10. Open Problems and Future Directions

Scalability Constraints

Training trillion-parameter LLMs requires exa-scale computational resources. Techniques like mixture-of-experts (MoE) [54] and pipeline parallelism [351] are critical but need further optimization.

10. 开放问题与未来方向

可扩展性限制

训练万亿参数LLMs需百亿亿级计算资源。混合专家（MoE）[54]与流水线并行[351]等技术至关重要，但仍需优化【百亿亿级计算（Exa-scale Computing）：每秒百亿亿次浮点运算的超算能力[351]】。

Ethical Alignment Risks

Current alignment methods may fail to prevent subtle harmful behaviors. Research on red teaming [352] and adversarial training [353] aims to close these gaps.

伦理对齐风险

现有对齐方法可能无法阻止隐蔽有害行为。红队测试[352]与对抗训练[353]研究致力于填补此类漏洞【红队测试（Red Teaming）：通过模拟攻击评估模型安全性的方法[352]】。

11. Conclusion

This survey systematically reviews post-training advancements in LLMs, emphasizing their evolution from task-specific tuning to integrated reasoning systems. Future work must address scalability, safety, and multimodal coherence to unlock LLMs' full potential.

11. 结论
本文系统综述了LLMs后训练技术的进展，强调其从任务特定调优到集成推理系统的演进。未来需突破可扩展性、安全性与多模态连贯性以释放LLMs的全部潜力【多模态连贯性（Multimodal Coherence）：跨模态信息保持逻辑一致性的能力[50][326]】。
